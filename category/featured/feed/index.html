Factors That Influence the Payout Amounts

The performance of the athletes is another crucial factor. The participants earn money based on their rankings in each round of the NFR. The higher they rank, the larger their share of the prize money.

This system encourages consistency and excellence throughout the competition and rewards those who consistently perform at the highest level.

Moreover, individual event sponsors may contribute the prize money for specific categories, which can impact the total payout for those events.

The NFR payout system is a fair and transparent structure that ensures the athletes are duly rewarded for their exceptional skills and achievements.
