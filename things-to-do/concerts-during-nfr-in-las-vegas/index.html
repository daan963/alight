Concerts During NFR 2021 in Las Vegas

If you’ve never been to the Wrangler National Finals Rodeo (NFR) in Las Vegas, this is an event you must attend, at least once in a lifetime. 2021 marks the 37th year for the Wrangler NFR to be held in Las Vegas. Don’t miss all the action before and after the NFR each night at the Cowboy Corral located at the Cox Pavilion, next to the Thomas & Mack Center. Get to the arena an hour early and head over to the Cowboy Corral. Keep up your heels and enjoy live entertainment.
