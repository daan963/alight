Pikes Peak or Bust Rodeo 2023: Schedule and how to watch online

by Jeffrey James July 7, 2023 0 17 Views
Share:

The Pikes Peak or Bust Rodeo is an annual event held in Colorado Springs, Colorado. What sets it apart is its stunning location at the base of the majestic Pikes Peak mountain, part of the Rocky Mountains range. Standing at over 14,000 feet above sea level, Pikes Peak provides a breathtaking backdrop that adds to the excitement of the rodeo.

Pikes Peak or Bust Rodeo
Venue Norris Penrose Event Ctr, Colorado Springs, CO
Start Date Tuesday, July 11th
End Date Saturday, July 15th
Time 7:00pm
Broadcast TCC+/TCC

With a history dating back to the early 1900s, the Pikes Peak or Bust Rodeo has a rich heritage. It originated in 1937 as a fundraiser for the local Chamber of Commerce and has since grown into one of the largest and most renowned rodeos in the country. It attracts top competitors from around the world while staying true to its Western roots and commitment to the community.

About The Rodeo

The upcoming 2023 event will span four days, from July 11th to July 15th, featuring daily rodeo performances and special events. The rodeo enjoys a strong partnership with the PRCA (Professional Rodeo Cowboys Association), ensuring the participation of top athletes and maintaining professional standards. Last year, the inaugural NFR Open replaced the RAM National Circuit Finals held in Kissimmee, Florida. Known for its lucrative nature, the event rivals the Reno Rodeo in terms of rewards. More than 200 contestants from all 12 U.S. rodeo circuits and Canada will gather at the Norris-Penrose Event Center, competing in five rounds over the course of the four days.

The Pikes Peak or Bust Rodeo is an extraordinary experience that combines the thrill of rodeo events with the natural beauty of the surrounding landscape. It continues to captivate audiences and leave a lasting impression on all who attend.

Schedule Highlights

Thrilling Rodeo Performances

The Pikes Peak or Bust Rodeo delivers top-notch rodeo action, making it a must-see event on the west coast. Enjoy both matinee and evening performances, providing ample opportunities to satisfy your rodeo cravings. For evening performances, the grounds open at 4:00 p.m., with the rodeo commencing at 7:00 p.m. Matinee performances start at 12:00 p.m., with the grounds opening at 10:00 a.m. Parking is free, adding convenience to your rodeo experience.

Exciting Mutton Busting

A major highlight for kids is the exciting mutton busting event, held daily. Preliminary rides occur at 4:00 p.m. from Tuesday to Saturday nights, and at 10:00 a.m. for the Friday and Saturday matinees. The finals take place during each rodeo performance, adding an extra level of anticipation and excitement.

Fan Zone & Vendor Extravaganza

If you arrive early, take the opportunity to explore the lively Fan Zone and browse through a variety of vendors. The event offers much more than shopping, with pony rides, longhorns, a barrel train, a petting zoo, roping demos, and even a horse-drawn wagon ride. Immerse yourself in the vibrant Western atmosphere before the rodeo action begins.

Sustainability and Environmental Commitment

While celebrating Western culture and tradition, the Pikes Peak or Bust Rodeo is conscious of its impact on the surrounding environment. Organizers prioritize sustainability by implementing practices such as recycling, composting, and water conservation. Collaborating closely with the City of Colorado Springs and local organizations, the event takes steps to minimize its environmental footprint.

Ticket Information

Tickets for the 2023 Pikes Peak or Bust Rodeo are now available for purchase. Secure your tickets conveniently online or visit the Norris-Penrose Event Center to make your purchase. Ticket prices vary depending on the day and seating area, offering options to accommodate every budget. Don’t miss out on this unique and thrilling event that showcases the best of Western culture and tradition. We hope to see you there!
