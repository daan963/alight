Who Competes at the Rodeo?

The San Antonio Rodeo is sanctioned by the Professional Rodeo Cowboys Association (PRCA) and the Women’s Professional Rodeo Association (WPRA).

The PRCA and WPRA rodeo seasons begin on October 1 and end September 30 each year.

Contestants are eligible to enter based on the below criteria:

PRCA

  • Top 45 contestants in each event in the 2022 PRCA World Standings
  • Top 5 contestants in each event in the 2023 PRCA World Standings

WPRA

  • Top 4 contestants from 2023 WPRA Pro Rodeo Standings
  • Top 32 from 2022 Pro Rodeo Standings
  • Top 4 from the 2022 WPRA Tour Standings
  • Top 10 contestants from the San Antonio Rodeo Qualifier

WPRA Breakaway Roping

  • Top 10 contestants from 2023 WPRA Breakaway Roping World Standings
  • Top 40 from 2022 WPRA Pro Rodeo Breakaway World Standings
