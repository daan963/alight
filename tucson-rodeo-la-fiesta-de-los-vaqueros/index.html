Tucson Rodeo 2023: How to watch La Fiesta De Los Vaqueros online

Tucson Rodeo is back for 2023! The 98th Annual Tucson’s La Fiesta De Los Vaqueros Rodeo will be held on Saturday, February 18th – Sunday, February 26th, 2023 in Tucson, AZ. The first La Fiesta de los Vaqueros (Celebration of the Cowboys) in 1925 touted 3 days of events and competition. Today, the event has grown to a 9 day celebration centered on the Tucson Rodeo, one of the top 25 professional rodeos in North America. Where does the Tucson rodeo take place?

The Tucson Rodeo Grounds
The Tucson Rodeo Grounds is a 36-acre facility which is the home of La Fiesta de los Vaqueros Tucson Rodeo and the Tucson Rodeo Parade and Museum.

Who competes in the Tucson Rodeo?

The Tucson Rodeo is a professional rodeo which invites contestants from the United States and Canada! This is one of the first major events on the PRCA schedule!

How long does the Tucson rodeo last?

The Tucson Rodeo enlists contestants from the United States and Canada for the nine-day celebration, which is known for “non-stop action with bull riding, bareback and saddle bronc riding, steer wrestling, and barrel racing.”

How much are Tucson Rodeo tickets?

Tucson Rodeo tickets for the event at Tucson Rodeo Grounds, 4823 S. 6th Ave, Tucson, AZ are on sale now and it will take place on February 26th, 2023 at 12:00pm. 314 tickets are currently available for the event on TicketSmarter’s app and website with prices in the range of $37.00 to $85.00.

What time is the rodeo parade in Tucson?

This colorful, exciting Parade is presented each year and keeps the spirit of the first Parade back in 1925. Now known to be the largest Non-Motorized Parade in the country, It still achieves its success through the community’s participation and the dedicated work of its volunteer members. The Parade begins at 9 am.
