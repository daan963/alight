Cave Creek Rodeo Days 2023 schedule

At this time, the Cave Creek Rodeo will not be open to the public to be in compliance with CDC guidelines and the Governor’s executive order. Stay tuned to official website and Facebook page as things change daily!

Thursday, March 23, 2023

  • 5:00PM – Gates Open at the Cave Creek Memorial Arena.
  • 5:00PM-10:00PM – Vendors Open at the Cave Creek Memorial Arena
  • 7:30PM – All Bulls, All Night!

Friday, March 24, 2023

  • 5:00PM – Gates Open at the Cave Creek Memorial Arena.
  • 5:00PM-10:00PM – Vendors Open at the Cave Creek Memorial Arena
  • 7:30PM – First PRCA Rodeo Performance.

Saturday, March 25, 2023

  • 5:00PM – Gates Open at the Cave Creek Memorial Arena.
  • 5:00PM-10:00PM – Vendors Open at the Cave Creek Memorial Arena
  • 7:30PM – Second PRCA Rodeo Performance.
  • 9:00PM – Official Cave Creek Rodeo Days Dance. Sponsored by Harold’s Corral in Cave Creek, Arizona

Sunday, March 26, 2023

  • 9:00AM – Cowboy Church at the Cave Creek Memorial Arena.
  • 12:00PM – Gates Open at the Cave Creek Memorial Arena.
  • 12:00PM-4:00PM – Vendors Open at the Cave Creek Memorial Arena
  • 2:00PM –Third and Final PRCA Rodeo Performance.
