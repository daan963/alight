World Darts Championship 2023/24: Schedule, Draw, Format, Qualifying, Prize fund & Odds

December 4, 2023 by Herbert

The world of darts is gearing up for the 31st edition of the World Darts Championship, set to take place at the iconic Alexandra Palace in December 2023. In this article, we’ll dive into the schedule, draw, qualifying process, and the odds that add an extra layer of excitement to this thrilling event.

When is the World Darts Championship?

The battle for the coveted Sid Waddell trophy kicks off on December 15, 2023, and concludes on January 3, 2024. Save the dates as we witness the world’s best dart players compete for glory.

Qualifying Process

Understanding how players qualify adds another layer of intrigue to the championship. With 32 players earning direct entry based on the PDC Order of Merit, 32 more secure their spots through the 2023 PDC Pro Tour Order of Merit. The final 32 players earn their place through diverse international routes, showcasing the global reach of darts.

World Darts Championship Format

The format evolves as the competition progresses. From best-of-five sets in the initial rounds to best-of-seven in the third and fourth rounds, the intensity builds. The quarterfinals shift to best-of-nine sets, followed by best-of-11 sets in the semifinals. The grand finale is a best-of-13 sets showdown, ensuring a thrilling climax to this prestigious tournament.

Prize Fund

Darts isn’t just about precision; it’s also about substantial rewards. The Professional Darts Corporation offers a staggering £500,000 to the winner, forming part of a £2.5 million total prize fund. Let’s break down the rewards at each stage:

  • Winner: £500,000
  • Finalist: £200,000
  • Semifinalist: £100,000
  • Quarterfinalist: £50,000

The World Darts Championship 2023/24 promises unparalleled excitement, combining skill, strategy, and nerve-wracking competition. Whether you’re a seasoned fan or a newcomer to the world of darts, this event is set to captivate audiences worldwide.

FAQs

How are players selected for the World Darts Championship?

Players earn entry based on their ranking in the PDC Order of Merit, the PDC Pro Tour Order of Merit, and through various international routes.

What is the format of the championship matches?

Matches progress from best-of-five sets to best-of-13 sets in the final, with increasing intensity in later rounds.

What is the total prize fund for the World Darts Championship?

The total prize fund is an impressive £2.5 million, with the winner receiving £500,000.

Who are the favorites to win the championship according to bookmakers?

Luke Humphries, Gerwyn Price, and Michael van Gerwen are among the top favorites according to betting odds.
