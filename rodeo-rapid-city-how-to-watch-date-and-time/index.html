Rodeo Rapid City 2023: How to Watch, Date and Time

Rodeo Rapid City is back for 2023! The Annual Rapid City PRCA Rodeo will be held on Friday, January 27th – Saturday, February 4th, 2023 in Rapid City, SD. This Rodeo Rapid City is held at The Monument and hosted by Sutton Rodeo. Here is your guide to all of the 2023 events at the Rapid City, SD rodeo in the continental US.

Rodeo Rapid City 2023
Venue The Monument, Rapid City, SD
Date Jan 27 – 29, 2023
Sanction PRCA, WPRA
Purse $88,000
Broadcast The Cowboy Channel, CowboyChannelPlus

Main Events

  • Bareback Riding
  • Saddle Bronc Riding
  • Bull Riding
  • Tie-down Roping
  • Team Roping
  • Steer Wrestling
  • Barrel Racing

Broadcast nationwide and nominated multiple times as the Top Five Large Indoor Rodeo of the Year, with four title wins remembering consecutive wins for 2020 and 2021, Rodeo Rapid City has turned into a significant stop making progress toward the National Finals Rodeo. This is a chance to see a world class professional rodeo!

What TV Channel will the Rodeo Rapid City 2023 be on?

The Cowboy Channel is the official media partner for the PRCA. Because this is an exclusive deal between the network and the PRCA, the Rodeo Rapid City will not be shown on any other TV network during the “4x PRCA Top Indoor Rodeo of The Year” period.
